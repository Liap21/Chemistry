Thermodynamics

Thermodynamics is the study of energy and heat transfer in chemical systems.

It deals with concepts like entropy, enthalpy, and Gibbs free energy, which provide a framework for predicting whether a chemical reaction will occur spontaneously and the direction it will take.

Kinetics

Kinetics focuses on the rates of chemical reactions and the factors that influence them.

It helps chemists understand the mechanisms by which reactions occur and how to control reaction rates through variables like temperature, concentration, and catalysts.
